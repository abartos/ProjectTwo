A sessionable beer for hopheads and commoners alike. The lemon citrusy hop profile is noticeable but pleasant, thanks to loads of whirlpool and dry hop additions.

Loonacy

A Belgian Strong Pale Ale
Available Seasonally in the Taproom

Belgian Pilsen Malt, US Sterling Hops, and Belgian Ale Yeast produce a moderate bitterness, low hop aroma, a light body and nice Belgian notes. Careful 8.0%!


Patio Pils

A Pilsner
Available Seasonally in the Taproom

OMNI's first lager! This is an american take on the Bohemian Pilsner style; Sterling Hops, Floor Malted Bohemian Pilsner Malt, and Czech lager yeast create a light-medium bodied, moderately bitter beer for summertime on the patio. OMNI's brand new patio to be precise.


Grateful Graf

A Malted Cider
Available Seasonally in the Taproom

A slightly sweet, slightly tart malted cider. A blend of cider and ale yeasts create a light bodied thirst quencher.


Outside the Loop

A Biere de Garde or French Farmhouse Ale
Available Seasonally in the Taproom

Our collaboration with our friends at Spilled Grain Brewhouse in Annandale. A French malt backbone combine with local honey from Schmidt's Honey Farm to create a truely unique malt forward brew. Biere de garde was traditionally brewed in modern day France near the Belgium border. We hope you enjoy this take on one of the few French styles of beer.

Oktoberfest

A Marzen Style Lager
Available Seasonaly in the Taproom

​ OMNI's Oktoberfest is a classic Marzen/Oktoberfest style lager. We added biscuit malts to enhance the pleasant breadiness. Prost!


Soaked 2016 MN Cascade

A Fresh Hop Ale
Available Seasonally in the Taproom

​ Soaked 2016 is made with 100% Golden Promise malt and fresh Cascade hops grown by Mighty Axe in Ham Lake, MN just 25 miles from OMNI Brewing. These hops are so fresh they traveled from the bine to the kettle in less than 6 hours! This fresh hopped beer is a unique experience that only comes around once a year.

Unite!

An Imperial Stout
Available limitedly in the Taproom

Unite! Is a reminder that in a world of divisive politics and bad news pushed to our pockets constantly, we still have more in common than not. Very dark caramel malt and malted oats combine for complex flavors of caramel, roastiness, and black licorice. Unite! is truly a communal libation.
